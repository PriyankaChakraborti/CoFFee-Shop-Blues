This displays the calculated Utility Scores for each neighborhood. These are created by averaging over the Utility Score for all coffee shop in the neighborhood. Higher scores indicate that coffee businesses thrive better in that specific neighborhood. For neighborhoods with between 1 and 9 coffee shops in the Yelp database, neighborhood scores were predicted using Lasso Regression and available third-party information.
