Neighborhood Clustering Comparison

Below is the main plot I am trying to show on this page.
This shows the 20 neighborhoods judged to be most similar to the chosen neighborhood, and displays their similarity to each other. Scores range from Zero to Three. 0 indicates neighborhoods which are never clustered together and Three means they are put in the same cluster by all 3 clustering algorithms. Clustering algoirthms used are K-Means Clustering, GMM Clustering, and Dendrogram Clustering. The higher the score the more confident we should be that they have similar coffee culture.
