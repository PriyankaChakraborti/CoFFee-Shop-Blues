What is this?

This helps coffee lovers and coffee-shop owners make informed decisions about which neighborhoods to explore or where to set up a business.

Neighborhood Map

This shows the utility score for each neighborhood. This represents how strong the coffee culture is. It also allows you to visually see the density of coffee shops in the area.

Neighborhood Clusters

This allows you to select a particular neighborhood. It then finds 19 other similar neighborhoods and compares all using multiple clustering algorithms to determine just how similar their coffee cultures are.

Positive/Negative Words

This shows the top 10 words most important to the positive or negative Yelp Reviews for the chosen city. These are decided by using NLP and an SVM.

Future Plans

In the future I want to also incorporate game theory to add recommendations for what type of options to coffee shop should include to be most successful in its neighborhood.
